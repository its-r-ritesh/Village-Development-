About Us

Our Mission



Village Development's mission is to enhance livelihoods and empower communities through infrastructure development, education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. Infrastructure development includes roads, bridges, water supply systems, sanitation facilities, and electricity.

Education and healthcare services are also important for sustainable development.

Economic empowerment includes promoting agriculture, supporting small-scale industries, encouraging local entrepreneurship, and exploring opportunities in sectors such as tourism.

Our Vision



Rural development is the process of improving the quality of life and economic wellbeing of people living in villages. It aims to achieve overall growth in the rural areas in terms of health, education, and quality of life.

The Rural Development Department's vision is to improve the socio-economic conditions of the rural community and to uplift the people living below the poverty line.

The department's programs are meant for poverty alleviation, reduction of unemployment, or to give additional employment to people living in rural areas.



Our Values
  • Community-driven development
  • Sustainability
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Equality and inclusivity
